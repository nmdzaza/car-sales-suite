What's Inside

4 Powerful Skills

Each skill is a focused AI workflow built for a specific part of the car sales process.

📋

dealer-onboarding

One-time setup that saves your profile so every future session is pre-personalized with your dealership details.

💬

car-sales

Works paid, warm, and dead leads. Builds outreach sequences, logs your pipeline, and sends iMessages directly.

🔍

buyer-leads

Finds people actively looking to buy by scanning Reddit, Facebook, Craigslist, and Twitter in real time.

🎯

conquest-leads

Identifies private sellers you can flip into trade-in and upgrade deals — turning competitors into customers.

How It Works

Up and Running in Minutes

Install once, then just talk to Claude. Your profile is remembered across every session.

Install the Plugin

Use the one-command install in Claude Code, or manually clone the repo and copy it into your plugins folder.

Run Onboarding

Claude automatically starts dealer-onboarding and asks 8 quick questions to personalize your experience.

Find Leads

Activate buyer-leads or conquest-leads to surface hot prospects from social and classifieds.

Close Deals

Let car-sales build personalized outreach, track your pipeline, and fire off iMessages automatically.
