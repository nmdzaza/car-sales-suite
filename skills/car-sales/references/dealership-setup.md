## Training the Sales Team (30–60 Min Session)

### What to Cover

**1. The 3 inputs they'll give every time (5 min)**
- Customer name + contact info
- Lead type (hot / warm / dead / paid)
- Car link from their inventory page

That's it. AI does the rest.

**2. How to review a draft before sending (5 min)**
- AI always shows the message before it goes out
- They read it, make any changes they want, say "send" or "edit"
- Never goes out without their eyes on it

**3. Live demo — working one real lead (15–20 min)**
- Pull up an actual dead or warm lead from their CRM
- Input it into the system
- Watch the AI pull the car details from their website link
- Review the draft message together
- Send it (or save it if they prefer)

**4. The follow-up sequence — set it and check it (10 min)**
- Show them the 5-step cadence (Day 1, 2, 5, 10, 21)
- Explain: system drafts each one, they just approve and send
- No more leads falling through the cracks

**5. Q&A (10 min)**

## Common Questions from the Team

**"What if the customer's car link is from a different site (AutoTrader, etc.)?"**
The system can pull data from any public car listing URL — dealer site, AutoTrader, Cars.com, CarGurus. Just paste the link.

**"What if the car sold and we want to send a different one?"**
Input the new car link. The system builds the whole package around whichever link you give it. Easy swap.

**"Can I customize the message before it sends?"**
Yes, always. The draft is shown before anything goes out. Edit it however you want — change the tone, add something personal, remove a section.

**"What about leads who only want to text and not email?"**
iMessage only is fine. Skip the email steps in the sequence.

**"What if we don't have financing partner info yet?"**
Use placeholder text like "We work with multiple lenders to find you the best rate" until they confirm their partners.
