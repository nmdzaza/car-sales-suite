# Follow-Up — When the Lead Responds

When a lead replies to an outreach message, the associate inputs what the customer said and the AI drafts the next response immediately. Speed here is critical — a response within 5 minutes dramatically increases the chance of closing.

---

## How to Use This

Associate inputs:
1. What the customer said (copy/paste their reply)
2. Any new info they gave (budget, trade-in, questions, objections)
3. What the associate wants to accomplish with the next message (schedule visit, answer question, send more info, close)

AI generates: the reply draft + suggested next step.

---

## Response Scenarios

### "I'm interested — tell me more"

This is a buying signal. Send the full car package immediately.

```
Hey [FIRST NAME]! Awesome, I'm glad it caught your eye.

Here's everything on the [YEAR] [MAKE] [MODEL]:

[CAR SUMMARY BLOCK — pull from car-package.md]

[FINANCING BLOCK]

[TRADE-IN BLOCK if applicable]

Best next step — come take it for a spin. I can have it ready for you [TODAY / TOMORROW / THIS WEEKEND]. What time works best?

📞 [SALESPERSON PHONE]
```

---

### "What's the best price you can do?"

Don't discount over text. Bring them in.

```
Hey [FIRST NAME] — I appreciate you asking directly, I'll be straight with you.

The [PRICE] is competitive for what this car is, but I always want to make sure the deal makes sense for you. The best way for me to work with you on numbers is in person — I can see what we can do on your trade, your financing, and any dealer incentives that might apply.

Can you come in [TODAY / THIS WEEK]? Even just 30 minutes. I'll make sure it's worth the trip.

[SALESPERSON NAME] | [PHONE]
```

---

### "What are the monthly payments?"

Give them the estimate and pull them toward an application.

```
Great question [FIRST NAME]. Based on the price of [PRICE], here's a rough idea:

• 48 months: ~$[CALC]/mo
• 60 months: ~$[CALC]/mo
• 72 months: ~$[CALC]/mo

These are estimates — your actual rate depends on credit and down payment. The good news is we work with [FINANCING PARTNER 1] and [FINANCING PARTNER 2] and can usually find a payment that works.

Want to get pre-qualified? It's quick and doesn't hurt your credit:
👉 [FINANCE APPLICATION LINK]

Or just come in and we'll run it together on the spot.
```

---

### "I have a trade-in"

This is great news — use it to anchor the deal.

```
That's actually really helpful [FIRST NAME] — trade-ins can make a big difference on your monthly payment.

What are you driving? Year, make, model, and rough mileage if you know it. I can give you a ballpark right now, and then we'll do a formal appraisal when you come in.

Or you can get an instant estimate here:
👉 [TRADE-IN LINK]

Either way, bring it with you when you visit and we'll factor it straight into the deal.
```

---

### "My credit isn't great"

Never make them feel judged. Position the dealership as a solution.

```
Hey [FIRST NAME] — I appreciate you being upfront, that actually helps me help you better.

We work with people in all kinds of credit situations — that's part of what we do. [FINANCING PARTNER 1] and [FINANCING PARTNER 2] both specialize in finding options for people who've had some bumps in the road.

The honest answer is I won't know what we can do until we run your numbers — and that's a 5-minute process. No pressure, no judgment.

Want to come in and let us take a look? Worst case we tell you where you stand. Best case, you drive home in the [YEAR] [MAKE] [MODEL] today.
```

---

### "I need to think about it" / "Not ready yet"

Don't push. Plant a seed and set a soft follow-up.

```
Totally understand [FIRST NAME] — it's a big decision and I don't want to rush you.

I'll check back in with you [IN A FEW DAYS / NEXT WEEK] just to see where your head is at. In the meantime, the car is still available and I've got your info on file.

One thing worth knowing — inventory moves fast and I can't guarantee it'll still be here, but I'll do my best to flag you if anything changes.

Talk soon 👊
[SALESPERSON NAME]
```

Set a reminder to follow up in 3–5 days. Go back to the sequence in `references/outreach.md`.

---

### "I already bought somewhere else"

Don't burn the bridge — this person knows other buyers.

```
Hey [FIRST NAME] — congrats on the new car! That's exciting.

No hard feelings at all — I'm just glad you got taken care of. If you ever need anything down the line, or if someone you know is in the market, I'd really appreciate the referral.

[DEALERSHIP NAME] | [SALESPERSON NAME] | [PHONE]

Hope you love it! 🚗
```

---

### Objection: "I saw it cheaper somewhere else"

```
Hey [FIRST NAME] — I hear you, and I appreciate you giving me a chance to work with you before making a decision.

Can you send me what you're seeing? I want to make sure we're comparing apples to apples — mileage, condition, what's included. Sometimes the price difference shows up elsewhere in the deal.

If they're genuinely cheaper on the same car, I'll tell you straight. But if there's a way to match it or come close, I will. Let me know what you're looking at.
```

---

## After Every Response

Once the lead replies and the associate sends the follow-up:
- Update lead status (Hot → In Conversation → Appointment Set → Closed / Lost)
- If appointment is set: send a confirmation text 1 hour before
- If no appointment: schedule next follow-up per the sequence cadence in `outreach.md`
