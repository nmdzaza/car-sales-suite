# Buyer Outreach Templates

Templates for reaching active buyers found via Reddit, Craigslist Wanted, Twitter, OfferUp, and for reactivating denied/dead leads. Every message is personalized to their specific situation — reference exactly what they said or posted.

Always show the draft in the review block before sending.

---

## Tone Guide by Buyer Type

| Buyer Type | Tone | Lead With |
|------------|------|-----------|
| Active shopper (Reddit/CL) | Helpful, knowledgeable, no pressure | Answer their actual question first |
| Subprime buyer | Warm, empathetic, solution-first | "We specialize in situations like yours" |
| Denied lead reactivation | Friendly, time-aware, optimistic | "Things may have changed since we last spoke" |
| Dead lead reactivation | Casual, low pressure, value-first | A new reason to look now (rate, inventory, season) |

---

## REDDIT REPLY TEMPLATES

These are public replies to Reddit posts. Keep them short, helpful, and transparent. Never pitch hard — Reddit users hate salesy comments. Add value first, then mention the dealership.

---

### Active Shopper — Reddit Reply

*(Post: "Looking for a reliable family SUV under $20k in Miami")*

```
Hey [username] — [SALESPERSON] here, I work at [DEALERSHIP] in [CITY].

For that budget in that range, a few that come up reliably:
- [OPTION 1 — real suggestion based on their stated needs]
- [OPTION 2]
- [OPTION 3]

We actually have a [YEAR MAKE MODEL] in stock right now that fits what you're describing — [BRIEF SPEC HIGHLIGHT]. Happy to send you the details if you want to compare it against what you find.

No pressure at all — just want to make sure you have real options.
[SALESPERSON] | [DEALERSHIP] | [PHONE]
```

**Key:** Answer their question with real suggestions FIRST. The pitch is the last thing, not the first.

---

## SUBPRIME BUYER TEMPLATES

For people who posted about bad credit, being denied, or needing second-chance financing.

---

### Subprime — Reddit Reply

*(Post: "520 credit score, need a car for work, got denied at two dealers")*

```

520 is workable depending on the down payment and the lender. We use [LENDER NAME] who focuses specifically on credit-challenged buyers — they look at the full picture, not just the score.

A few things that help: time on the job (even a few months), any down payment you can put together, and having your income documents ready.

Happy to run a soft check if you want to see where you'd stand — won't affect your credit. Just DM me or call: [PHONE]

[SALESPERSON] | [DEALERSHIP]
```
